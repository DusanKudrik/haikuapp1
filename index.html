Haiku Diary

About

Haiku Diary is a quiet place to save short poems, small thoughts, and daily moments.

A traditional haiku is often written in three simple lines:

5 syllables

7 syllables

5 syllables

Use it as a daily poem journal, a small memory keeper, or a calm writing habit.

Private by design

Haiku Diary does not require an account. Your haikus are saved on this device and are not uploaded anywhere by the app.

For safekeeping, you can export a backup file from Settings.
